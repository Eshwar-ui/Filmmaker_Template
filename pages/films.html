The Forgotten Shore

2024
Feature Length

The Forgotten Shore

2024 · 94 min

A coastal community fights to preserve their way of life against rising seas.

Sundance IDFA Winner Hot Docs

Synopsis

In a remote fishing village on the Atlantic coast, a community of 200 families faces the reality that their ancestral home is disappearing beneath the waves. Shot over eighteen months, "The Forgotten Shore" follows three families as they navigate the impossible choice between holding onto everything they know and accepting an uncertain future inland.

Director's Statement

"I arrived in this village expecting to make a film about climate change. Instead, I found a story about love — the love for a place, for a way of life, for the sound of waves that have been a lullaby for generations."
