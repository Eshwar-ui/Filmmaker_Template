Services

Documentary Services

End-to-end documentary production for brands, broadcasters, non-profits, and independent producers. From concept and research to final delivery, each project is built for cinematic quality and distribution readiness.

What We Offer

Core Service Lines

Development

Story research, treatment writing, interview strategy, and production planning before cameras roll.

Field Production

On-location directing, cinematography, and sound capture in controlled or challenging environments.

Post-Production

Narrative editing, color finishing, sound design, subtitle support, and final master prep.

Festival Delivery

Submission packaging, screener strategy, press assets, and exhibitor-ready technical outputs.

Process

How Projects Move Forward

Step 01
Discovery Session
Define goals, target audience, and distribution pathway to align creative and business outcomes.
Step 02
Production Blueprint
Build schedules, crew plan, permits, interview shortlists, and risk contingencies.
Step 03
Filming & Capture
Capture principal footage and cinematic b-roll with clear narrative intent.
Step 04
Editorial & Delivery
Edit, finish, review, and deliver masters optimized for festivals, streaming, or social rollouts.
